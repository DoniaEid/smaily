About Us

Clinic story

                    The SMAILY clinic started with a clear vision: providing a different, comfortable, and safe tooth treatment experience for every patient. From the first day,
                    our goal was to draw a smile, not only on the faces, but also in the hearts of people. We relied on the latest medical technologies, and we care about the
                    smallest details, so that each visit is distinguished .

Doctors team

                    Our team consists of a group of specialized dentists, who are unanimously gathered by the field and high experience. Every doctor in SMAIY is interested
                    in hearing the patient, simply explaining his condition, and provides him with appropriate treatment with the highest quality. We work as one team, and
                    our goal is always the patient's comfort and satisfaction .

Our message and our vision

                    Message: Providing an integrated health care of the teeth in a friendly and safe environment, using the latest devices and technologies, and with
                    international standards.
                    vision: We are the first destination for medicine and cosmetics in the region, and we build the confidence of each patient from the first visit.
